CLASSIC STRATEGY GAME

REVERSI

Place your pieces carefully, surround your opponent, and take control of the board.

How to Play

Place a piece so that one or more opponent pieces are trapped between two of your pieces.

The trapped pieces flip to your colour. The player with the most pieces at the end wins.
